=== MEDICAL SPECIALTIES ===

CARDIOLOGY
- Heart diseases, hypertension, arrhythmias
- Symptoms: chest pain, shortness of breath, palpitations

NEUROLOGY
- Brain, nervous system disorders
- Symptoms: headaches, dizziness, numbness, seizures

GASTROENTEROLOGY
- Digestive system disorders
- Symptoms: stomach pain, nausea, diarrhea, constipation

ORTHOPEDICS
- Bones, joints, muscles
- Symptoms: joint pain, fractures, sprains

PULMONOLOGY
- Respiratory system, lungs
- Symptoms: cough, shortness of breath, chest pain

DERMATOLOGY
- Skin conditions
- Symptoms: rashes, itching, skin lesions

GENERAL MEDICINE
- Common illnesses, fever, infections
- Symptoms: fever, cough, cold, body aches

=== COMMON VITAL SIGNS REFERENCE ===

BLOOD PRESSURE
Normal: < 120/80 mmHg
Elevated: 120-129/<80 mmHg
High: >= 130/80 mmHg

HEART RATE
Normal adult: 60-100 bpm
Abnormal: < 60 or > 100 bpm

TEMPERATURE
Normal: 36.5-37.5°C (97.7-99.5°F)
Fever: > 38.5°C (101.3°F)
Low: < 36°C (96.8°F)

OXYGEN SATURATION
Normal: 95-100%
Low: < 90%

=== COMMON MEDICINES ===

ANTIBIOTICS: Amoxicillin, Azithromycin, Ciprofloxacin
PAIN RELIEF: Paracetamol, Ibuprofen, Aspirin
HYPERTENSION: Lisinopril, Amlodipine, Metoprolol
DIABETES: Metformin, Insulin, Glibenclamide
